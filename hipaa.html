HIPAA Data Handling Agreement

Effective Date: [Insert Date]

This HIPAA Data Handling Agreement describes responsibilities for handling protected health information and other sensitive data submitted to [Company Name] through its platform and services.

1. Authorization and Permitted Use

You represent that all healthcare-related data submitted through the platform is submitted with proper authorization and may be used for legitimate billing, claims, recovery, and operational support activities.

2. Safeguards

The Company maintains administrative, technical, and physical safeguards designed to protect confidential information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure.

3. Access Controls

Access to sensitive records is restricted to authorized personnel and systems with a business need-to-know. Logging, role controls, and policy-based restrictions are applied where appropriate.

4. Incident Response

The Company will investigate suspected security incidents and provide notice where required by law, contract, or applicable compliance obligations.

5. Business Associate Agreement

Where required, the parties will execute a separate Business Associate Agreement (BAA) or equivalent legal document.

6. Client Responsibilities

  • Submit only authorized information.
  • Use secure account credentials and role-based access internally.
  • Comply with applicable federal and state privacy and security laws.
